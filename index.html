Terms Summary

  • New Customer Booster: If more than 60% of ER comes from new customers, an additional 3% of ER will be paid as incentive.
  • Ancillary Charges: Any sort of ancillary or value-added charges will be included in NRV calculation.
  • Minimum Monthly Performance: A BDM must achieve minimum 50% of both NRV and ER targets in at least two out of the three months within the evaluation quarter to qualify for quarterly incentive.
  • Exception: If a BDM overachieves (i.e., exceeds 100% of NRV & ER targets) in one month, but fails to meet the 50% threshold in the remaining two months, incentive will be paid only for that one month.
